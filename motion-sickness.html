Motion Sickness First-Aid
  • Help the person to sit in a comfortable, Close your eyes,well-ventilated area
  • Use an anti-nausea patch called the Scopolamine Transdermal Patch
  • Provide the person with fresh air by opening a window or turning on a fan
  • If possible, stop the vehicle or the motion that is causing the sickness
  • If the person's symptoms do not improve or they have other symptoms, such as severe vomiting or headache, seek medical attention
