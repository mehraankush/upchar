Diabetes First-Aid
  • If the person is conscious and able to swallow, provide them with a sugary drink or candy to raise their blood sugar level
  • If the person is unconscious or unable to swallow, do not give them anything to eat or drink and seek medical attention immediately
  • Check the person's vital signs and monitor their breathing and level of responsiveness
  • If the person has a glucagon kit, administer it as directed
  • If the person's condition worsens or they become unresponsive, call emergency services or take them to the nearest hospital for further treatment
