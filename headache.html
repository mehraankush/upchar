Headache First-Aid
  • Help the person to rest in a quiet, dark room
  • Offer the person a cool compress or ice pack to the affected area
  • If the person has medication for their headaches, help them to take it if they need it and if it is safe to do so
  • Encourage the person to take slow, deep breaths and to relax their muscles
  • If the person's headache does not improve or if they have other symptoms such as a fever or neck stiffness, seek medical attention
