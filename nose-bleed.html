Nose bleeding First-Aid
  • Help the person to sit down and lean forward slightly to avoid swallowing blood
  • Pinch the soft part of the person's nose with a clean cloth or tissue and apply gentle pressure for at least 10-15 minutes
  • Apply a cold compress to the person's nose to constrict blood vessels and reduce bleeding
  • If bleeding persists or the person feels faint or dizzy, seek medical attention
  • Encourage the person to avoid blowing their nose or picking at any scabs for at least 24 hours
