Burns First-Aid
  • Remove the source of heat or flames and move the person away from danger
  • Cool the burned area with cool (not cold) water for at least 10-20 minutes to reduce pain and swelling
  • Cover the burn with a clean, dry, non-adhesive dressing or cloth to protect it from infection
  • If the burn is severe, seek medical attention immediately
  • If the person's clothing is stuck to the burn, do not try to remove it. Cut around the burned area and remove any loose clothing
